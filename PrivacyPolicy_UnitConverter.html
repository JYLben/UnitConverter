1. Overview

Unit Converter is an Android application that allows users to convert between various measurement units (length, weight, area, volume, temperature, and currency). The app operates locally on your device except when retrieving current currency exchange rates from a third-party public API.

2. Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No account creation, analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers are used.

3. Network Access

For currency conversions, the app connects to the Frankfurter API (https://www.frankfurter.app) to retrieve public exchange rates. Only minimal, non-personal data (the request for exchange rates) is transmitted. We do not send or store any user data on external servers.

4. Permissions

The app requires only standard Android permissions necessary for network access (android.permission.INTERNET). No access to your files, contacts, camera, or location is requested.

5. Data Security

All conversions occur locally on your device. When the app connects to the Frankfurter API, data is transmitted over a secure HTTPS connection.
